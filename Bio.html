About Rebecca Vest

Rebecca Vest is currently attending the University of Maryland Baltimore County as a Visual Arts Major with a concentration in Graphic Design and heavy credits in Photography. Inspired by her grandmother who was a painter, Rebecca was first picked up a camera at 11 years old and hasn't put it down since. She loves working in traditional mediums as well as with the digital world. Rebecca also really likes cats.
